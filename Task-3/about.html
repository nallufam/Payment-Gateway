Culture


The Sparks Foundation (India) has a workplace culture that aligns with its ‘knowledge-sharing philosophy. The organization defines the workplace environment as diverse and multicultural, which contributes to a productive atmosphere.
Most employees are friendly and helpful, but freshers shouldn’t expect too much guidance. As a startup, the workload can be overwhelming with tight schedules.


Mission and values


The organization desires a ‘world of enabled and connected little minds’. Its objectives focus on three aspects: inspire, innovate, and integrate.
